About Us

WHAT IS LEARN BLUE

Learn Blue Org, Formerly Teen Media Geek Project is an organisation aimed at creating awareness on the UNSDGs with primary focus on the SDGs, 4, 8 and the Migration Crisis most youth encounter when looking to travel. We having taking up the decision to create awareness on the global pressing issues such as lack of quality education, migration crisis and the infamous climate change and global warming. We work on bridging the gap between employment opportunities and teenagers.

OUR STORY - AS TOLD BY OUR FOUNDER

Initially, Teen Media Geek Geek as formerly called was inspired by my experience of getting the opportunity to work remotely as a web development teacher for Skillshare and LearnFly Academy as teen who had recently finished high school, I wasn't necessarily asked for certifications, they simply saw a content on one of my social media accounts and decide to take chances... We made it our mission to ensure that teenagers and youth alike have an equal chance on employment opportunities as long as they possess the skillset required for the given position with or without any formal (university) education. The decision to rename and re brand and reexaming came during the 2019 electioneering, I had noted a news interview where a reporter asked residents of their expectations of the new government and they all literally replied the same thing, like "Infrastructure, Power and 'Education'".

Not a single individual had focused or mentioned that the government addressed the issue of global warming across the various state of the country, then it dawned on me that it's most likely these individuals knew not the impact of global warming and climate change despite them experiencing it's already adverse effect daily, because if they did/do, they might have as well mentioned that as an expectation from the government, they should have called on the attention of the said minister of environment on his actions or implementations put in place for tackling climate change amongst other environmental issues.

Nigeria has the highest maternal mortality in the world. It's the country with highest number of extremely poor people in the world and now, it has the highest burden of fatalities from air pollution in Africa and 4th globally? Nigeria also currently has the highest burden of fatalities from air pollution in Africa and 4th highest in the world with 150 deaths per 100,000 people attributable to pollution according to a research report. Personally I realized my allergies to fumes and fumigants, after the very busy traffic in the city of Lagos each day, trying to get to work, I feel sick each time I took the day off, the accumulated polluted air, from exhaust burning bushes etc I had inhaled had settled within my respiratory system and left me very Ill.

Ever since then, I had taken it upon myself to empower teenagers like myself to take actions against climate change, global warming and other forms of environmental injustice.
