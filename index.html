Who We Are

LEARN BLUE -
REVISIONING TOMORROW

We are diverse group of young adults who are changing the African teen narrative by enlightening them on the Sustainable Development Goals and empowering them with basic skills needed in taking action towards, combating climate change, fostering decent jobs and economic growth and ending migration crisis

Our Goal

We aim to foster teen inclusiveness in taking action towards achieving the sustainable development. To build a community of teenager who are mobilized to use their youthful resources - time and strength, their love for social media for sustainable development.

About Us

Learn Blue Org, Formerly Teen Media Geek Project is an organisation aimed at creating awareness on the UNSDGs with primary focus on the SDGs, 4, 8 and the Migration Crisis most youth encounter when looking to travel. We having taking up the decision to create awareness on the global pressing issues such as lack of quality education, migration crisis and the infamous climate change and global warming. We work on bridging the gap between employment opportunities and teenagers.
